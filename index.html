Hey there, Alee!

Welcome to your own little corner of the Internet.

As we go through struggles in our daily lives, it's easy to become stressed out and nervous about the future.

Sometimes you wish you could just hit "pause" on life and just not think or worry about anything, right?

My hope is that Mechie's Corner allows you to do just that - pause life, just for a moment, and catch your breath.

I've included a meditation space for when you're feeling anxious, a "hall of memories" for when you're sad, and a collection of calming "flow" activities for when you're bored and overthinking things.

Think of this space as a garden, where you can plant seeds of joy that grow into flowers of peace in your everyday life.

Stay as long as you like.

Come back often.

And always remember that I love you.
