MECHIE'S SOUL GARDEN

Anxious?

Sad?

Bored?

Hey there, Alee!

Welcome to your own little corner of the Internet.

As we go through struggles in our daily lives, it's easy to become stressed out and nervous about the future.

Sometimes you wish you could just hit "pause" on life and not have to think or worry about anything, right?

My hope is that Mechie's Soul Garden allows you to do just that - pause life, just for a moment, and catch your breath.

I've included a meditation space for when you're feeling anxious, a "hall of memories" for when you're sad, and a collection of calming "flow" activities for when you're bored and overthinking things.

Treat this like a real garden: plant seeds by spending time here, wait a little, and watch those seeds grow into flowers of patience and peace in your real, everday life.

Stay as long as you like.

Come back often.

And always remember that I love you.
